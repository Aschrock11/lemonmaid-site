How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Columbus, Ohio?

Quick answer: Standard house cleaning in Columbus usually costs $100-$200. Deep cleaning typically starts at $180-$280 based on size and condition.

What Affects Cleaning Prices?

Price depends on home size, number of bathrooms, clutter level, time since last clean, and add-ons like oven interior or interior windows.

Typical Columbus Price Ranges

  • Standard cleaning: $100-$200
  • Deep cleaning: $180-$280+
  • Move-out cleaning: $200-$350+
  • Airbnb turnover: $80-$150+

How to Get the Best Value

Choose recurring service after your first deep clean, keep add-ons focused on the highest-need areas, and compare insured/local companies instead of just lowest price.
