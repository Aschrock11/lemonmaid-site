Deep Cleaning vs. Standard Cleaning: What's the Difference?

Standard cleaning maintains cleanliness. Deep cleaning resets the home by tackling details regular cleanings skip.

Standard Cleaning Includes

Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom sanitizing, and routine surface/detail wipe-downs.

Deep Cleaning Adds

Inside windowpanes (inside only), hand-detail baseboards, tile/grout detail cleaning, wall spot-checking, and hood vent detailing.

Inside cabinets/drawers and inside fridge/freezer/stove are included with Move-In/Move-Out cleaning.

When to Choose Deep Cleaning

Choose deep cleaning for first-time service, seasonal reset, move-in/move-out, or after long gaps between cleanings.
